  • Information about Volcanoes
    • This is a page about volcanoes. Volcanoes are fascinating and cool in nature. Here, you will see a picture of a volcanoe. A link to my resume. Also, a link to a youtube video about volcanoes. You will also find a Tableau graph that you will be able to interact with.
    • Volcanoes are openings in the Earth's crust that form when lava, gases, and debris build up and erupt onto the surface.
    • Volcanoes can be found on every continent, including Antarctica. The United States has about 170 volcanoes, including active and dormant volcanoes.
    • Eruptions can last for days, months, or years. They can release acid, gases, rocks, and ash into the air.
